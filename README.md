I'm an **Analytics Engineer** focused on making data **trustworthy** — through rigorous testing, clean modeling, and documentation that enables confident, business-critical decision-making. My work lives at the intersection of engineering rigour and business clarity — transforming messy raw data into clean, trusted metrics that teams can actually use.

I build analytics systems with three things in mind:
1. **Trust** — every metric is tested, documented, and explainable
2. **Scalability** — models that grow with the business without breaking
3. **Clarity** — clear naming, clear lineage, clear ownership
